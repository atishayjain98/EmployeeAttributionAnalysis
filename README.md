# EmployeeAttributionAnalysis

# Problem Statement

Employee attrition refers to employees leaving a company, which can lead to increased hiring costs, loss of experience, and disruption in operations. Attrition analysis helps organizations understand the reasons behind employee turnover and take proactive steps to improve retention.

# Objective

- Explore historical HR data to understand who’s working at the company, what they do, and who’s leaving.
- Spot early signs of attrition by looking at things like tenure, satisfaction, and promotions.
- Find out why people leave by identifying if they feel overworked, stuck or disengaged.
- Provide HR with clear insights to create better support and keep employees motivated.
